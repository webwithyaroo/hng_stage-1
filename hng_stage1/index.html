My Tech Goals for the Next 2 Years

Goal 1: Mastering JavaScript Frameworks

In the next two years, I aim to master popular JavaScript frameworks such as React and Vue.js. These frameworks are pivotal in modern web development, enabling the creation of dynamic and responsive user interfaces. I plan to dive deep into advanced concepts, contribute to open-source projects, and build several complex applications to solidify my understanding and proficiency.

Goal 2: Building a Strong Portfolio

I intend to create a robust portfolio showcasing a variety of projects that demonstrate my skills and versatility in frontend development. This will include personal projects, freelance work, and contributions to community initiatives. A well-rounded portfolio will not only highlight my technical abilities but also my problem-solving skills and creativity.

Goal 3: Enhancing Design Skills

While my primary focus is on development, I recognize the importance of design in creating appealing and user-friendly interfaces. Over the next two years, I plan to improve my design skills by studying UI/UX principles, experimenting with different design tools, and following the latest design trends. This will help me create more visually appealing and intuitive websites.

To become a full-stack developer, I will dedicate time to learning backend technologies. Understanding how the server-side of web applications works will allow me to create more comprehensive and integrated solutions. I will start with Node.js and Express, and gradually explore other backend frameworks and databases.

Goal 5: Contributing to the Community

I believe in the power of community and the importance of giving back. In the next two years, I plan to contribute to open-source projects, write technical articles, and participate in coding forums. Sharing knowledge and helping others will not only improve my skills but also build a network of like-minded professionals.
